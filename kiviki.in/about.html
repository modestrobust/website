About Us

Welcome To KIVIKI

KiViKi is a brand which stands for natural, fresh, healthy and pure. KiViKi is fun, innovative and has integrity.

It is a nutrient dense promise of health and goodness, born out of the vision to provide a hassle free, affordable and authentic way to consume truly pure and freshly prepared cold pressed juices within the comfort of one’s home.

Kiviki’s juices are 100% fresh and natural with no sugar, preservatives, water or anything artificial. Fresh juices are made every morning and delivered to the customers’ doorstep the same morning. Daily juicing model also ensures that freshest fruits and vegetables are procured daily.

KiViKi wants to be a part of customers’ daily lives with a subscription model, which ensures consistency and commitment. The menu has been designed keeping in mind various aspects of health and subscribing to all the juices means all round nourishment of the body and spirit.
