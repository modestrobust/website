About Us

What does cold pressed mean?

Regular juicers at home or juice-bars separate the juice from the fruits using fast-spinning blades leading to loss of vitamins & nutrients. Cold-pressed juicers don’t have blades. The fruits and vegetables are crushed and pressed to draw out the juice with minimal heating and create a nutrient-rich beverage. Cold pressed juice is super packed with nutrients and vitamins that go straight to your bloodstream for that immediate jolt of feel-goodness in the form of energy.
